## Overview

This app provides a safe, low-stakes environment where users can practice sales conversations with AI-powered customer personas, receive comprehensive feedback, and track their progress over time.

## Main Features Walkthrough

### 1. Scenario Selection & Chat Interface

**"Let's start by navigating to the Chat page."**

When you first open the app, you're presented with **five predefined sales scenarios**, each featuring:
- A distinct customer persona with unique personality traits
- Product information you'll be selling
- Specific challenges and success criteria

For example, you might be selling a SaaS productivity app to an indecisive buyer, or handling a warranty upsell with an annoyed customer.

**"Once you select a scenario, you enter a real-time chat interface where you're practicing your sales pitch."**

---

### 2. Interactive Conversation with AI Customer

**"Here's where the magic happens - you're having a real conversation with an AI customer persona."**

**How it works:**
- You type your responses as the salesperson
- The AI customer responds in character, adapting their personality, concerns, and buying behavior based on the scenario
- The conversation feels natural and realistic because the customer responses are generated by **Llama 3.3 70B Instruct** (`@cf/meta/llama-3.3-70b-instruct-fp8-fast`), a large language model running on Cloudflare Workers AI

**"Notice this audio button here - you can click it to hear the customer's responses read aloud, which helps simulate a more realistic conversation experience."**

**Technical detail:** The AI customer persona responses are generated by **Llama 3.3 70B Instruct** (`@cf/meta/llama-3.3-70b-instruct-fp8-fast`) running on Cloudflare Workers AI. The model receives dynamically generated prompts that include scenario-specific customer persona instructions. Each scenario has customized instructions that tell the AI how to behave - whether they're skeptical, indecisive, technical, or impatient. The AI also remembers the conversation history (retrieves last 5 messages from the D1 database), so responses build naturally on what was said earlier.

---

### 3. Voice Input Feature

**"You can also use voice input - just click the microphone button and speak your response."**

The app uses the browser's native Web Speech API to transcribe your voice to text, making the practice feel even more natural and helping you work on your verbal delivery, not just written responses.

---

### 4. Ending the Scenario & Feedback Generation

**"After you've had your conversation, you click 'End Scenario & Get Feedback'."**

**What happens in the backend:**

This triggers a sophisticated **3-step AI analysis pipeline**, where each step uses **Llama 3.3 70B Instruct** (`@cf/meta/llama-3.3-70b-instruct-fp8-fast`) with specialized prompts:

**Step 1 - Transcript Analyzer Agent:**
- Uses **Llama 3.3 70B** with a specialized prompt for high-level conversation analysis
- The AI analyzes your entire conversation at a high level
- It identifies strengths and weaknesses in your communication
- It segments the conversation into meaningful parts (like opening, objection handling, closing attempts)
- The model receives scenario context (customer profile, product info, success criteria) to provide contextualized analysis

**Step 2 - Summary Analyzer Agent:**
- Also uses **Llama 3.3 70B** with a different specialized prompt focused on performance evaluation
- Generates overall performance ratings across **8 key dimensions**:
  - Empathy
  - Clarity
  - Assertiveness
  - Persuasion
  - Active Listening
  - Objection Handling
  - Closing Ability
  - Flexibility
- Provides narrative feedback on what you did well and areas for improvement
- Takes the segmented analysis from Step 1 and creates comprehensive summaries

**Step 3 - Detail Agent:**
- Again uses **Llama 3.3 70B** with a prompt specialized for phrase-level annotation
- Creates phrase-level annotations
- Highlights specific moments in the conversation
- Provides actionable suggestions for each segment
- Uses the segments from Step 1 to provide detailed, contextual feedback

**"All three agents use the same Llama 3.3 70B model, but each has a specialized system prompt that guides it to perform its specific analysis task. This happens sequentially in about 30-60 seconds, all powered by Cloudflare Workers AI."**

---

### 5. Comprehensive Feedback Report

**"Once the analysis completes, you're taken to a detailed feedback report with three main sections:"**

**Summary Tab:**
- Overview of the conversation
- Key themes and outcomes

**Evaluation Tab:**
- Your scores across all 8 performance dimensions
- Visual progress bars showing your strengths and areas for growth
- Narrative analysis of your performance

**Annotations Tab:**
- An annotated transcript of your conversation
- Highlighted segments with inline comments
- Specific suggestions for improvement

---

### 6. Interactive Feedback Chatbot

**"One of my favorite features is the built-in chatbot on the report page."**

You can ask questions about your feedback, like:
- *"Why do you say I wasn't personable enough here?"*
- *"What could I have done differently in this objection?"*
- *"How can I improve my closing technique?"*

The chatbot uses **Llama 3.3 70B Instruct** (`@cf/meta/llama-3.3-70b-instruct-fp8-fast`) - the same model used throughout the app - with a specialized prompt that acts as a sales coach. It has access to your entire feedback report, so it can provide contextual, specific answers that help you understand and learn from the analysis.

**Technical detail:** This uses the same Llama 3.3 70B model with a specialized "feedback coach" prompt. The model receives your feedback report, annotated transcript, and your question, then generates personalized guidance based on the specific context of your performance.

---

### 7. Scenario Progression

**"After reviewing your feedback, you can navigate to the next scenario."**

The app cycles through all five scenarios, allowing you to practice different types of sales situations:
- Different customer personalities
- Various product types
- Different difficulty levels

Each scenario provides new challenges and learning opportunities.

---

### 8. Home Dashboard - Progress Tracking

**"Finally, let's look at the Home page, which serves as your progress dashboard."**

Here you can see:
- **Overall session statistics** - how many scenarios you've completed today
- **Average scores** across all your practice sessions - showing your improvement over time
- **Session history** - a collapsible list of all your past practice sessions

When you expand a session, you can see:
- Conversation summary
- Performance analysis
- Suggested improvements

This gives you a comprehensive view of your progress and helps identify patterns in your performance.

## Technical Architecture

**"Let me briefly explain the technical architecture:"**

This app is built entirely on the **Cloudflare Developer Platform**:

- **Frontend**: React 19 with TypeScript, deployed on Cloudflare Pages
- **Backend**: Cloudflare Workers - serverless functions that handle all API requests
- **AI**: Cloudflare Workers AI with **Llama 3.3 70B Instruct** (`@cf/meta/llama-3.3-70b-instruct-fp8-fast`) for all AI operations:
  - Customer persona responses (chat)
  - Transcript analysis (Step 1 of feedback pipeline)
  - Summary generation (Step 2 of feedback pipeline)
  - Detail annotations (Step 3 of feedback pipeline)
  - Feedback chatbot responses
- **Database**: Cloudflare D1 (SQLite-based edge database) for storing conversations, messages, and feedback
- **Session Storage**: Cloudflare KV for temporary session state
- **Streaming**: Server-Sent Events (SSE) for real-time AI responses

**Why Cloudflare?**
- **Global edge distribution** - low latency worldwide
- **Cost-effective scaling** - pay only for what you use
- **Integrated platform** - all services work seamlessly together
- **Serverless architecture** - no servers to manage
